About Sharia

I Am Just the
Messenger.

My story is the message.

Hey sis. I'm Sharia — a 33-year-old single mom of two who was married and divorced before 21, who lost her mother and sister in 2023 while a 5-year relationship ended the same year with no closure.

I know what it's like to wake up with that heavy weight in your chest, wondering how you got here and if you'll ever find you again. For years, I prioritized keeping the peace over protecting my purpose — until I realized that God didn't call me to be a martyr for a cycle of chaos.

I didn't find healing through willpower. I found it by understanding the science of why our brains stay stuck, and then anchoring that science to the Word of God. That is what I teach. That is what I live. And now I'm doing it from Italy — one way ticket, two kids, and a whole lot of faith.

I am just the messenger for the message God gave me. And today, that message is for you.
